About Us

We started with the sole aim of breaching the gap between quality Nigerian meals prepared and served to international standards. Evidently, we had big dreams. Nevertheless, we started small with a bakery and a single restaurant in Port Harcourt. The rest they say is history as we have outlets everywhere now.
